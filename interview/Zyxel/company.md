The target position is under CTO / COE software and focuses on next-generation XPON solutions.

Based on the job description, the role likely involves:

- Linux user-space network application development

- Automation

This is closer to a **Linux Networking / Embedded System Software Engineer** role than a traditional MCU firmware role.
